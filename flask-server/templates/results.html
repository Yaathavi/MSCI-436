Understanding Utility Scores

The utility scores are calculated to provide a personalized ranking of universities based on your preferences. Each criterion is given a weight based on its importance to you, and the overall utility score is a combination of these weighted scores. Hereâ€™s a brief explanation of each criterion:

  • Overall Utility Score: The total score combining all criteria weights.
  • Admission Averages Utility: Score based on the admission averages.
  • Tuition Cost Utility: Score based on the tuition costs.
  • Satisfaction Utility: Score based on student satisfaction ratings.
  • Employment Rate Utility: Score based on the employment rates of graduates.
  • Retention Rate Utility: Score based on the proportion of students who continue their studies (retention rate).
